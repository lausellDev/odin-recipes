Grilled Corn on the Cob

We love corn!!! and the simplest way to prepare it is grilled but it dont understimate it you will love it also

Ingredients

  • 6 ears corn
  • 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Peel back corn husks and remove silk.
  3. Place 1 tablespoon butter, salt and pepper on each piece of corn. Close husks.
  4. Wrap each ear of corn tightly in aluminum foil.
  5. Cook on the preheated grill, turning occasionally, until tender, about 30 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
